Who are we?

UNIT Factory (Ukrainian National IT Factory) — Ukraine's only educational institution that works by innovative standards of French School “42”. Here you can learn how to code, enhance your team working skills and advance your creativity by looking for unique solutions. We don’t have teachers, work-books or lectures. Your academic degree or your grades are not taken into account during the application. Everything depends only on your abilities and motivation. And the education is completely free for all students.
