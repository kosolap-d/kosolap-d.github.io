One of the main advantages of Ukraine - it's human potential. To realize this potential it is necessary to educate young generation of professionals who are in great demand at the labor market nowadays, and will be even in bigger demand in 5-10 years. Experts of the labor market and futurists believe that the most promising professions are related to digital technologies.

UNIT Factory aims to give young people opportunity of getting well-paid jobs, thus help Ukraine to develop an innovative economy in condition of tough competition with other countries.

Ukrainian IT companies need professionals with an outstanding level of training to successfully compete on international arena. Technical universities and numerous programming courses graduates often do not meet this requirement, so employers are forced to teach or re-educate them.

UNIT Factory provides qualified IT professionals training that can satisfy Ukrainian companies personnel shortage. Unique project-based learning system forces it’s graduates to adapt to real-world commercial work as much as possible. We also help to form a community of young IT entrepreneurs who will glorify Ukraine in the world.

Young people need opportunities. IT sector provides a lot of them. You only need to be persistent and purposeful while studying.

UNIT Factory aims to help young people to get a profession with opportunities for continuous professional development. We want out graduates to be in demand at the labor market and engaged at interesting and ambitious projects of modern digital age.

Study principles

Peer-2-peer

UNIT Factory is open for graduates and students and for those who already have a profession, but want to change it. There is only one requirement: age. You must be from 17 to 30 years old to enroll. Education is completely free of charge for all students.

Our goal is to find the most talented and motivated students who may become high-level professionals. During the application process academic criteria (grades, academic degree etc.) are not taken into account. Programming skills also don’t have any decisive role. According to the experience of School 42 those students who had never developed software but had high potential quickly caught up with those who were already familiar with programming.

Curriculum

The curriculum - aims to elaborate the development skills of high quality software. UNIT Factory prepares students to work in leading IT companies. The purpose of the curriculum, is not only acquiring technical knowledge, but also getting practical experience in programming. It covers a wide range of areas, so students can select the most interesting ones for them.
