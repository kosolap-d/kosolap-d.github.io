Who we are?

UNIT Factory (Ukrainian National IT Factory) — єдиний в Україні навчальний заклад, що працює за стандартами інноваційної французької Школи 42. Тут навчають програмувати, працювати в команді та знаходити неординарні рішення. У нас немає вчителів, конспектів або лекцій. Під час відбору не враховуються дипломи або результати ЗНО. Все залежить лише від ваших здібностей і мотивації. І навчання безкоштовне для всіх студентів.

Study principles

Peer-2-peer

UNIT Factory is open for graduates and students and for those who already have a profession, but want to change it. There is only one requirement: age. You must be from 17 to 30 years old to enroll. Education is completely free of charge for all students.

Our goal is to find the most talented and motivated students who may become high-level professionals. During the application process academic criteria (grades, academic degree etc.) are not taken into account. Programming skills also don’t have any decisive role. According to the experience of School 42 those students who had never developed software but had high potential quickly caught up with those who were already familiar with programming.

UNIT Factory aims to give young people opportunity of getting well-paid jobs, thus help Ukraine to develop an innovative economy in condition of tough competition with other countries.

Curriculum

The curriculum - aims to elaborate the development skills of high quality software. UNIT Factory prepares students to work in leading IT companies. The purpose of the curriculum, is not only acquiring technical knowledge, but also getting practical experience in programming. It covers a wide range of areas, so students can select the most interesting ones for them.

Result

Education at UNIT Factory takes up to three years. After graduating from UNIT Factory you will have the knowledge, skills and portfolio necessary to work at such positions:

Software Architect
Technical Lead
Network Systems Administrator
Networks Architect
Web developer
Game developer
Specialist in IT security
Mobile app developer

FAQ

Should i know to swim to get to UNIT Factory throught "pool"?

“Pool” is a trial period of study lasting one month. Students who have successfully passed the “Pool” are enlisted to UNIT Factory. You won’t have to swim in it but you'll definitely have to dive into programming.

On the one hand the “Pool” will select the most talented and motivated students for UNIT Factory. On the other - during this month you will understand if the programming and education at UNIT Factory in particular fits you in general.
